You Have Chosen:

The Heir

As you are the late Tarquin McAllister's only living next of kin, his estate has fallen into your possession. You have decided to take stock of the house and its contents to see whether it can be renovated and sold.

Before your arrival at the estate, you also received the following missive from your late uncle:

Nephew,

If you are reading this message, it is because I have died. Know that as my last of kin, you stand to inherit the entirety of my estate--the house, and all its contents, are now yours. I care little for what you do with it all, as I am no longer alive and therefore have no use for any of it. Keep it. Sell it. Do with it as you like.

I write you this message as a warning. Something foul is afoot, and events that were set in motion years ago are at last coming to fruition. I am sure that many unsavory characters will be poking through the remains of my estate after my passing.

TRUST NO ONE

You have no involvement in all this, and do not deserve to be punished for my actions. If you are wise, you would do best to stay well away from the House.

Godspeed,

Tarquin McAllister

Abilities & Equipment

Family History

+1 to all insight checks on family-related matters.

Though you've never met your uncle, you've certainly heard stories about him from various family members.

Keyring

A set of keys to the House.

A large iron key ring, jangling with keys in a variety of shapes and sizes. Who knows what secrets these things will unlock?

Pocket Knife

1d4 piercing damage

Your trusty pocket knife. You mainly use it to open letters, but it's definitely sharp enough to hurt someone.
