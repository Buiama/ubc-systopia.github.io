Your Secret Role:

The Betrayer

You are Tarquin McAllister's trusted colleague from the Order of the Immutable Brethren, an organization dedicated to the preservation of life and the search for immortality. Though it has been a number of years since you had last spoken with Tarquin in-person, you recently received the a package from his estate.

The package contents were a letter addressed to you, a letter addressed to Tarquin's nephew, and a vial of blood. The letter addressed to you reads as follows:

Trusted Colleague,

If you are reading this message, it is because I have been betrayed. I have always known this day would come, and I am forever indebted to our Order of Immutable Brethren that I am prepared to face this setback.

I pass onto you, my most trusted colleague, a final request:

Deliver my phylactery to the ceremonial altar, located in the hidden basement of my estate. I have enclosed with this message a vial of my blood, which you must pour into the phylactery before placing it on the ceremonial altar.

I am in your debt, my friend. Rest assured that you will be adequately rewarded for your trouble.

Tarquin McAllister

Your Mission

Deliver Tarquin's phylactery to the ceremonial altar, located in the hidden basement of the House. To fulfill this goal, you must complete the following objectives:

  • Find a means of accessing the hidden basement
  • Find the phylactery
  • Place the phylactery + blood vial into the ceremonial altar in the hidden basement.
