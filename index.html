The best way to make sure you and your friends vote
 

Adulting is hard.
Voting shouldn’t be.

I spaced on the registration deadline. I forgot to mail in the absentee ballot. I don’t know enough about the candidates. These “behavioral microbarriers” too often get in the way of turning out to vote.

We make fun, easy and social — so you and your friends actually do it. motivote is the first peer-to-peer platform using gamification, social pressure and real-life rewards to engage and turn out young voters. And it works: 84% of motivote’s 18-29 year-old active users voted, compared with 31% of eligible voters nationwide.

How It Works

Pledge

Form or join a team. Commit to vote with friends. Optionally back it up with a financial pledge.

Nudge

Get informed and engaged with a custom roadmap and one-stop shop of resources. Earn points and prizes as you make progress.

Vote

Come election day, confirm you voted with an “I Voted” selfie that goes to your friends (and us).
