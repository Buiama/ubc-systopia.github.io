Partner With Us
 
For organizations and campaigns

Organization Partners

motivote empowers your supporters to engage and turn out their own networks. Leverage the power of relational organizing to reach young people where they actually are, motivated by friends they trust. We help you drive engagement, event attendance, and broader activism with friendly competition and rewards along the way.
For brands and influencers

Brand Partners

motivote’s innovative platform rewards users for taking steps that better prepare them to vote, from making a plan to researching candidates. That’s where our brand partners come in. Support civic engagement by sponsoring user rewards, from merchandise to experiences. You’ll get in front of civically engaged young voters and deepen your relationship with followers.
